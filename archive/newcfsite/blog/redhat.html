CloudFit Software LLC Named Red Hat Ready Business Partner

Media Contact:
Ellie O’Hara
CloudFit Software
434-534-0015
getfit@cloudfitsoftware.com

Lynchburg, VA – CloudFit Software, a managed service provider, today announced that it has been named as a Red Hat Ready Business Partner in the North America Public Sector. As part of Red Hat’s partner ecosystem, CloudFit has shown that they offer Red Hat certified expertise and are highly committed to working closely with Red Hat on business opportunities.

As a Red Hat Ready Business Partner, CloudFit Software is able to support successful implementation of Red Hat’s open hybrid cloud products, including Red Hat Enterprise Linux; Red Hat OpenShift; Red Hat OpenStack Platform; Red Hat Storage; and Red Hat Application Services. CloudFit Software offers the technical expertise and working practices to deliver a high degree of customer satisfaction across a range of deployment scenarios and projects.

CloudFit Software provides its customers several managed services that exist to help achieve success in their planning, implementation and operations of cloud-based applications and platforms that host a given customer’s critical business functions. CloudFit provides these managed services via three core areas of business: Managed Cloud Transformation, Managed Monitoring Experience, and Managed Application Development.

CloudFit Software’s managed services are underpinned by a proprietary technology platform aptly named CloudFit Software. The CloudFit Software platform enables complex business rules to be triggered by any number of system level inputs and pre-defined outputs and is constantly evolving to allow CloudFit Software to meet the ever-changing needs of its customer’s applications and platforms.

Supporting Quotes 

“We are excited about the collaboration with Red Hat, especially on the DevSecOps front.  As is the case in our relationship with Microsoft, the collaboration goes well beyond human services and consulting.  We are directly engaged with the Red Hat product teams as we aim to enable DoD on Azure and Azure Stack across all impact levels and at scale.”  

Carroll Moon, CTO & co-founder of CloudFit Software

“Red Hat’s partner ecosystem is a vital component in delivering powerful, flexible and open solutions to enterprises. We’re pleased to have CloudFit as a Ready Business Partner in the North America public sector, and look forward to delivering open innovation to our joint customers with them.” Michael Byrd, vice president, public sector channel sales, Red Hat

About CloudFit Software

Founded in 2018 by former Microsoft veterans and located in Lynchburg, VA, CloudFit Software was established to fill a need in the IT industry and is now leading the market in “Managed Scenarios” for cloud across Fortune 500, DoD and Regulated Industries.
