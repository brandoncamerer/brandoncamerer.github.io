CloudFit Software to appear on “World’s Greatest!…” TV Show

PRESS RELEASE
June 8, 2020

Contact:
Kate Byrd, CloudFit Software
katebyrd@cloudfitsoftware.com

CloudFit Software to appear on “World’s Greatest!...” TV Show #WorldsGreatestTV

Lynchburg, VA – CloudFit Software is pleased to announce that the company will appear on the “World’s Greatest!...” TV show on June 29, 2020 and July 20, 2020.  The exact TV schedule will be documented at this location: https://worldsgreatesttelevision.com/tv-schedule/.  Following both dates, the full episode will be available under Season 14 at the following location:https://www.worldsgreatesttelevision.com. 

Carroll Moon, CloudFit Software’s CTO, stated the following: “CloudFit Software is excited to further tell our story to the world.  Our mission is about more than IT.  We are excited for the episode to bring great visibility to the company, to our mission, to our charities (KidFit and JobFit), to Lynchburg, to the Lynchburg Regional Business Alliance, and to Liberty University.  The Lynchburg Regional Business Alliance has helped CloudFit Software in countless ways, and we currently occupy space in the Lynchburg Regional Business Alliance office.  Liberty University is a great partner with student internships and Capstone Projects, students who become Full Time Employees of CloudFit Software, and for training under the JobFit charity umbrella.”

Filming occurred at CloudFit Software’s offices inside the Lynchburg Regional Business Alliance (https://www.lynchburgregion.org/), Liberty University School of Business (https://www.liberty.edu/business/), KidFit (http://www.kidfitministries.org) and JobFit (http://jobfit.online/).

About CloudFit Software

CloudFit Software is leading the industry in “Managed Scenarios” for cloud across Fortune 500, DoD and Regulated Industries.  CloudFit Software’s primary place of business is Lynchburg, VA.  CloudFit Software has employees in 20 states.  CloudFit Software moves critical services to the cloud, creates critical services in the cloud and manages critical services (or subsets thereof) once they are in the cloud.  CloudFit Software exists to create and drive these scenarios for the IT industry, but CloudFit Software has a greater mission with its charities: KidFit (use athletics as a conduit to provide mentorship and opportunities for all kids regardless of their ability to pay) and JobFit (lowering the barrier to entry for high school students, college students and career changers for great IT careers).
