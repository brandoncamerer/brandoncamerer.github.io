Welcome to KidFit

KidFit, a non-profit organization, is dedicated to transforming lives one child at a time, from cities to states and nations. Our mission is to offer mentorship and inclusive opportunities to children of all backgrounds, fostering their growth both socially and athletically through skilled training and excellent coaching.

In collaboration with various organizations, KidFit brings camps and valuable opportunities to Lynchburg, with a current focus on mentoring at the Jubilee Center. Looking ahead, our long-term vision is to establish KidFit programs in every community, ensuring widespread support and enrichment for children everywhere.
