What is a CloudFitCoin (CFSC)?

CFSC is a internal cryptocurrency with a Solana base. (FitCoin and CFC were taken) ;)

Why does CFSC exist?

CFSC was created to be a fun way to interact with coworkers, trade in for prizes, trade NFTs, and encourage company camaraderie.

How was CFSC created?

CFSC was created (minted) following a YouTube tutorial by a YouTuber named NetworkChuck.

How do I get started?

Start by installing the Phantom App and creating a crypto wallet then simply send an email to CFSC@cloudfitsoftware.com your deposit code! Here is a link to a YouTube tutorial!

How do I trade CFSC?

To trade CFSC you will need a person's deposit code and some Solana. (send an email to CFSC@cloudfitsoftware.com for some free Solana)

How do I acquire more CFSC?

How do I refer a friend?
