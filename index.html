CloudFitCoin FAQ!

What is a CloudFitCoin (CFSC)?

CFSC is a internal cryptocurrency with a Solana base. (FitCoin and CFC were taken) ;)

Why does CFSC exist?

CFSC was created to be a fun way to interact with coworkers, trade in for prizes, trade NFTs (Coming soon), and encourage company camaraderie.

How was CFSC created?

CFSC was created (minted) following a YouTube tutorial by a YouTuber named NetworkChuck.

How do I get started?

Start by installing the Phantom App and creating a crypto wallet then simply ping Brandon Camerer your deposit code! Here is a link to a YouTube tutorial!

How do I trade CFSC?

To trade CFSC you will need a person's deposit code and some Solana. (ping Brandon Camerer for some free Solana)

What are the prizes?

Right now the prize is a CloudFit swag box for 500 coins. (More to come!)

Who uses CFSC?

Right now 12% of full time employees are participating in CloudFitCoin trading. (Don't forget! There is a +50 coin Refer-a-Friend bonus)

How do I acquire more CFSC?

To acquire more coins you can refer a friend (+50 coins), trade with friends or win some during our weekly raffle. (More ways to win coming soon)

How do I refer a friend?

To receive a refer a friend bonus (+50 Coins) simply have your friend mention you when they sign up.
